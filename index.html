Machang

Machang is your go-to spot for delicious, home-style Sri Lankan flavors in a warm and friendly setting. From spicy curries to crispy hoppers, every dish is packed with bold taste and made with love. Whether you're here for a hearty meal or just a quick bite, Machang promises comfort food that feels like home.

Hopper Night

Hopper Night is a special evening dedicated to enjoying Sri Lanka’s beloved traditional snack — crispy, bowl-shaped hoppers. Served plain or with tasty fillings like egg, coconut sambol, or spicy curry, Hopper Night is all about sharing good food and great vibes!
